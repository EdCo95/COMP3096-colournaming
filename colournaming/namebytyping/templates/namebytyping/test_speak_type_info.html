Speak/Type Test

You have been allocated the Speaking and Typing Test. In this test, you may either speak the name of the colour in the circle, or type the name in the textbox. Your computer will automatically listen for speech, once you have given it permission to use the microphone. If you decide to type a colour name, simply press enter to submit the answer. If you speak the name, then the answer will submit automatically.

Remember to speak clearly, but do not worry if the computer doesn't understand you correctly, for example it may think you said "dog" when you actually said "dark". If this happens, ignore it. Wrong interpretations are still valuable data about the experiment's accuracy!

If you cannot see the circle, there is a button underneath the image which you can click to skip the image. Finally, after 60 seconds with no answer, the speech recognition will turn off. If this happens, type the name of the colour for the image you're currently on, and as soon as you press enter and move to the next image, the speech recognition will restart. If the speech recognition is not working at all - i.e. 10 seconds after you say something no words have appeared in the textbox - then simply type the answers and ignore the speech recognition.
