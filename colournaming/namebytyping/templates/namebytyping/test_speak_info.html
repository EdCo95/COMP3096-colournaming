Speaking Test

This is a speaking only test. Your task is to speak the name of the colour in the circle, your computer will interpret your speech and will listen to you automatically, after asking your permission to use the microphone. Once you have spoken an answer, the image will automatically change.

Remember to speak clearly, but do not worry if the computer doesn't understand you correctly, for example it may think you said "dog" when you actually said "dark". If this happens, ignore it. Wrong interpretations are still valuable data about the experiment's accuracy!

If you cannot see the circle, there is a button below the image to click. In addition, if the voice recognition is not working - i.e. you say something but the text on the screen does not change for over 10 seconds - then there is a button you can click below the image. Finally, not saying anything for 60 seconds will cause the speech recognition to turn off. If this happens, a button will appear which you can click to restart it.

Click the "Practice" button to continue. You will be taken to a practice question before starting the real test so that you can try out the speech recognition.
