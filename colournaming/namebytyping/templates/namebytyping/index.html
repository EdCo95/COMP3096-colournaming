Colour Naming

This test will run for approximately 5 minutes. You will be shown a series of images, each of which has an area circled. Your task is to name the colour in that circle. Try to be as descriptive as possible, for example saying “lavender” instead of “purple” or “dark orange” instead of “orange”. If your browser is Google Chrome or Firefox you may (we will notify you if you can) be able to use your computer's microphone to name the colours by speaking them rather than having to type them. Before the test starts there are three questions to answer. All of your data is completely anonymous and no voice data from speech recognition is ever recorded - only the words that the computer thinks you said - nor is it possible for voice recognition to be switched on without your express permission. Granting permission to use voice recognition on this website will not grant permission to any other site.
