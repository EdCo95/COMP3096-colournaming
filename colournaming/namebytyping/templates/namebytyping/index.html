Colour Naming

This test will take approximately 5 minutes. You will be shown a series of images, each of which has an area circled. Your task is to name the colour in that circle. Try to be as descriptive as possible, for example saying “lavender” instead of “purple” or “dark orange” instead of “orange”. If your browser is Google Chrome or Firefox you may (we will notify you if you can) be able to use your computer's microphone to name the colours by speaking them rather than having to type them. Before the test starts there are three questions to answer. All data collected on this website is completely anonymous.
