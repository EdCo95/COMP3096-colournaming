Colour Naming

This test will run for 3 minutes. You will be shown one image at a time with an area circled. Your goal is to type the colour contained in the circle in each image. You should enter the first colour that comes to your mind but try to be as descriptive as possible, for example “lavender” instead of “purple” or “dark orange” instead of “orange”. Once the timer expires, you will be allowed to finish your answer for the current image.
